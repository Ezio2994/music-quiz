By selection 1 Player the game is going to start and once the song start playing you will be able to select the right answer with a click of the mouse, You have 10 seconds per track to answer, you will be asked first for the name of the track and secondly for the singer/group name, you get 5 points per every correct answer and lost 5 per every incorrect one.

By selecting 2 Players you can challenge a friend, player 1 will be able to select the right answer with a click of the mouse, while Player 2 will have to use the keyboard arrows, arrow left: Answer 1, up 2, down 3, right 4. The player who answer right first get the 5 points, if the player get it wrong lose the 5 points and the other player can still answer and get the points. Wins the player with more points.
